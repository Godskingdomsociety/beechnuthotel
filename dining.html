The Beechnut Restaurant

Step into a world where Nigerian culinary heritage meets contemporary refinement. Our restaurant offers an elevated dining experience — from sun-kissed breakfasts to intimate candlelit dinners — in a setting designed to delight. Every plate is crafted with seasonal, locally sourced ingredients, prepared by our executive chef and a passionate kitchen team.

Lobby Bar & Lounge

Unwind after a long day at our vibrant lobby bar, where master mixologists craft bespoke cocktails using premium spirits and house-made syrups.
